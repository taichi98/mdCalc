What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is used as a screening tool to identify possible weight problems in children. BMI is calculated from a person's weight and height.

Formulas

BMI (kg/m2) = weight (kg) / (height (m))2

BSA (m2) = √[(height (cm) × weight (kg)) / 3600]

Facts & Figures

BMI, kg/m2 Weight Category
< 18.5 Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 Normal weight
25.0 – 29.9 Overweight
30.0 – 34.9 Obese (Class 1)
35.0 – 39.9 Obese (Class 2)
> 40.0 Obese (Class 3)
